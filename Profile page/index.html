MLB's Most Talented Young Players

This is a list of the top three best major league players 25 and under.

In case you've been under a rock or disconnected from baseball in a whie, this is a list of three names you should know.

This list was created by six of our veteran sports analyst. They have developed this list of young stars by using performance stats from previous seasons(both major league and minor league).

The Three Players Are

Soto has led the majors in BB(base on balls) for four years consecutive. He has become one of the leagues premiere hitters in a short span of time. He is only 24 years old and has already proved to be a World Series winner 2019.

Soto, 24, has played six seasons in the big leagues. Five for the Nationals and one for the Padres. He was traded late last season after he turned down a max contract to stay in Washington. Soto is a free agent at the end of next season and is likely to be traded before the season ends. Many are interested but he will come at a high price.

Ronald Acuna Jr. is 25 and is the favorite to win the N.L. MVP award this season. He, like Soto, has won a World Series trophy. He is the first player in modern MLB history to reach the feet of 70 stolen bases and 40 home runs.

Acuna Jr. is an exciting five four tool player. He has played six years for the Braves and despite his injury history, he is one of the most exciting players in the league right now.

julio Rodriguez is in his second season of his career. He has played for the Mariners both years. He had a 30-for-30 seaon. He is only 22 years old and is already one of the American Leagues most feared hitters. Defensively his glove will earn him a gold glove this year.

Rodriguez has been selected to an all star game both of his seasons and has also won the rookie of the year award like the other two players on this list.
